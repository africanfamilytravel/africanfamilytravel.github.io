Frequently Asked Questions

What destinations do you offer in Africa

We specialize in Ghana and Kenya, offering a variety of experiences in these two beautiful African countries

Are your trips suitable for families with young children?

Yes, our trips are family-friendly and cater to a wide range of ages. We can tailor activities to suit your family's needs and preferences.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and preferences?

Absolutely. Please inform us of any dietary requirements or preferences when booking, and we will do our best to accommodate them

How do I book a trip with African Family Travel?

You can either select one of our pre-packaged travel experiences on our website or contact us for a custom-designed itinerary that suits your family./p>

What type of accommodations do you offer?

We offer a range of accommodations, from family-friendly hotels and eco-lodges to luxury resorts, depending on your preferences and budget.

What's the best time to visit Ghana or Kenya?

The ideal time to visit varies by destination and the experiences you're seeking. Contact us for the most up-to-date information based on your travel dates.

Do I need a visa for Ghana or Kenya?

Visa requirements vary by nationality. We recommend checking with the respective embassies or consulates for the most accurate and current information

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is detailed in the terms and conditions. Please review it before booking, and feel free to ask us any specific questions you may have

How do I make a payment for my trip?

Payment methods vary by location and the nature of your booking. We accept major credit cards and bank transfers. Details will be provided upon booking confirmation

What safety measures are in place during your trips?

Your safety is our priority. We follow all necessary safety and security protocols, and our guides are trained to provide a secure and enjoyable experience

How can I ensure my family's health while traveling in Africa?

We recommend consulting with a healthcare professional for up-to-date travel health advice, vaccinations, and any necessary medications.
